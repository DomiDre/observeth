About

Who we are and how to contact

The intention of this project is mainly to give everybody fast visual access to the information available on the open ethereum ledger. Having followed ether/token flows in multiple occassions by hand using etherscan or simple scripts, we realized it would be much easier if one could directly visualize the flow to directly observe any connections on first glance. For this purpose, we combined Metamask (easy access to data), vis.js (visualization library) and Angular to make an (hopefully) easy to use front-end. If you have any suggestions, feel free to contact us by mail or the donation guestbook.
